Course Animation (ANI)
Course type lecture
Lecturer Prof. Dr. Norbert Witt
Topics
  • Animation, Biovision Hierarchy, Motion Capturing (MoCap)
  • Writing and drawing of a screenplay and storyboard for a video sequence to the topic "Flying Angel / Superhero"
  • Recording the animations in the MoCap studio
Technologies Motion Capturing, OptiTrack
Tools ARENA, Blender
ECTS Credit Points 6 (together with Augmented Reality)
Examination type Presentation (1.3 MiB) with written composition (4.5 MiB)
Grade(s) 1.7 (together with Augmented Reality)
